\begin{frame}
  \frametitle{The local-to-global mapping}

  The global matrix $\iota_T$ is defined by
  \begin{equation*}
    I = \iota_T(i)
  \end{equation*}
  where $I$ is the \emph{global index} corresponding to
  the \emph{local index} i
