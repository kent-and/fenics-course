the Biot's equations with two pressures (solid $p_S$ and fluid $p_F$)
\[
\left[
\begin{array}{ccc}
-\nabla^2 & -\nabla & -\nabla \\
\nabla\cdot & -\frac{1}{\lambda} & 0  \\ 
\nabla\cdot & 0 &  -s + \nabla\cdot K \nabla   
\end{array}
\right]
\left[
\begin{array}{c} u \\ p_S \\ p_F \end{array} 
\right]
= 
\left[
\begin{array}{c} f \\ 0 \\ g \end{array} 
\right]
\]
