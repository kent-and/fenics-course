\begin{frame}
\frametitle{An implicit projection scheme, cont'd}
As for the explicit scheme, the equation 
\begin{eqnarray*}
u^c + \frac{\Delta t}{\rho} \nabla \phi &=& 0 \\ 
\nabla \cdot u^c &=& - \nabla \cdot u^* 
\end{eqnarray*}
may be written as 
\begin{eqnarray*}
-\nabla^2  \phi &=& -\frac{\rho}{\Delta t} \nabla \cdot u^* 
\end{eqnarray*}

\end{frame}
